inVOID is the new name of AI based SaaS solutions that can help your business set new industry standards.

In September 2018, the Supreme Court of India passed a judgement about section 57 of the Aadhaar Act, which drastically changed how verification processes by independent companies were to be carried out. While the Aadhaar could no longer be used extensively for online verification processes, inVOID decided to make sure that businesses did not have to go back to the tiring and mostly inefficient process of manual verification. So, inVOID reached out to humankind’s new best friend, Artificial Intelligence. There, we found our own unique solution by opening up the possibility of carrying out eKYC from end to end. All under a minute.

Enterprises saw a problem. inVOID saw endless technological possibilities.
